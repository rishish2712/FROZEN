About FROZEN SPOON

At FROZEN SPOON, we believe in crafting more than just ice cream; we create experiences. Our journey began in 2025 with a simple passion: to transform the finest, ethically sourced ingredients into extraordinary frozen delights. From the rich vanilla beans of Madagascar to the vibrant strawberries of local farms, every scoop tells a story of dedication and artistry.

We are committed to sustainability, quality, and innovation, constantly exploring new flavors and techniques to bring you unparalleled taste. Join us on a delicious adventure, one spoonful at a time.
